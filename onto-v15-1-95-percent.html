Everything you need to know about epistemic risk

Epistemic risk is the gap between AI confidence and actual accuracy. When an AI system states 94% confidence but is wrong, that's epistemic risk — and it creates legal liability, regulatory exposure, and real-world harm.

ONTO uses three core metrics: U-Recall (how well the system detects when it doesn't know), ECE (Expected Calibration Error — how well confidence matches accuracy), and Risk Score (aggregate liability indicator).
