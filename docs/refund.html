Refund Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

Summary: We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. Annual plans can be refunded pro-rata within the first 30 days.

1. Monthly Subscriptions

For monthly Pro plans:

  • 14-day guarantee: Full refund if requested within 14 days of initial subscription
  • No refunds for partial months after the 14-day period
  • You may cancel anytime; service continues until end of billing period

2. Annual Subscriptions

For annual Pro and Enterprise plans:

  • 30-day guarantee: Full refund if requested within 30 days of purchase
  • Pro-rata refund: After 30 days, refunds calculated based on unused months (minus 15% early termination fee)
  • No refunds after 6 months of the subscription term

3. Enterprise Plans

Enterprise agreements may have custom refund terms as specified in your contract. Contact your account manager for details.

4. Service Credits

If we fail to meet our SLA commitments:

  • 99.9% uptime breach (Pro): 10% credit of monthly fee
  • 99.99% uptime breach (Enterprise): 25% credit of monthly fee
  • Credits applied to next billing cycle, not redeemable for cash

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Accounts terminated for Terms of Service violations
  • Unused API credits or quota
  • Setup fees or custom integration work
  • Third-party fees (payment processing, etc.)

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Email billing@ontostandard.org with your account email
  • Include your reason for the refund request
  • We will respond within 2 business days
  • Approved refunds processed within 5-10 business days

7. Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. If that's not possible, we'll work with you to find an alternative.

8. Contact

Questions about refunds? Contact us at billing@ontostandard.org
