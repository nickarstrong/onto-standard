Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-04-27 · ONTO Standards Council · ontostandard.org

01 What we collect

ONTO Standards Council operates a registry, a verifier, and an attestation protocol. We collect only the data needed to operate these functions:

  • Account. Email, name, organization (when you register a provider account or council seat).
  • Model metadata. Model identifier, fingerprint, endpoint URL — published in the public registry as part of attestation.
  • Signed signals. Input hash, output hash, timestamp. We never receive or store the underlying inputs or outputs — only their hashes, signed by the provider.
  • Usage counters. Token counts and request counts for rate limiting; IP address retained 90 days for abuse detection.
  • /agent/ BYOK keys. Held client-side only. Never transmitted to ONTO servers.

02 What we don't collect

  • The text of any AI input or output. The protocol operates on hashes by construction.
  • Provider private signing keys. Providers hold their own keys; ONTO never sees them.
  • Consumer inference keys (OpenAI, Anthropic, xAI, etc.). When used in /agent/, these stay in the browser.

03 Sharing

We do not sell or share data with third parties for advertising or marketing. The public certificate page /cert/{slug} publishes only the provider name, model name, and current attestation status. Aggregate registry statistics may be published without identifying individual operators.

04 Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU, UK, or any jurisdiction with equivalent data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Request rectification of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion (subject to public registry retention requirements for issued attestations).
  • Request portability of your account data in a machine-readable format.
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest.

Send requests to security@ontostandard.org. We respond within 30 days.

05 Retention

  • Account data. Retained until you request deletion.
  • Signal metadata. Personally-identifying fields anonymized after 24 hours; attestation hashes themselves are part of the public chain and retained indefinitely.
  • Server logs. 90 days, then deleted.

06 Children

ONTO services are not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us and we will delete it.

07 Security

Account credentials are hashed. Signing keys for the attestation protocol use Ed25519. Transport uses TLS 1.2+. We do not promise unbreakable security — we promise that the attestation chain itself is independently verifiable, so a breach of our infrastructure does not invalidate past certificates.

08 Updates

Material changes to this policy will be announced by email to registered account holders and posted as a banner on this page for 30 days before taking effect. Non-material changes (typos, clarifications) take effect immediately.

09 Contact

Privacy and data protection requests: security@ontostandard.org

General council inquiries: council@ontostandard.org
