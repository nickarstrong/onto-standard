Terms of Service

Service. ONTO Standards Council operates a continuous attestation framework for AI models. Registered models stream signed signals (Ed25519) per inference. The Council records signal metadata only — never inference content. Public certificates are derived from signal aggregates and published at ontostandard.org/cert/{provider}.

Acceptable use. Registration is for genuine model attestation. Forging signatures, replaying signals, redistributing other accounts' signal streams, or representing the Council's certificate as a legal entity certification of your company is prohibited.

Privacy. Account fields (email, name, company) and signal metadata are stored under GDPR. Inference content is never transmitted to or stored by the Council. Account deletion removes records within 30 days; published certificates remain immutable in the public ledger.

Billing. Open tier baseline is always-on. Standard, AI Provider, and White-label tier rights are acquired per year and accumulate. Effective limits are computed across all active grants. No automatic renewals; expiry returns the account to Open baseline.

How SSE works
Your server keeps two channels with ONTO:
1. Heartbeat · you POST /v1/models/{id}/heartbeat every 30-60s · we mark you live
2. GOLD stream · you GET /v1/gold/stream · we push corpus updates · you cache & inject into your inferences
Throughput · bounded by your hardware (~30K-100K sig/sec typical) · No ONTO-side rate limit · No cost on your provider key
Channel notes
Proxy is fully supported for ongoing operations. SSE is recommended for new registrations because your provider key is never charged for inference and your server stays in the inference path.

Quick Start
Python — OpenAI-compatible (3 lines changed)
# Before (direct to OpenAI):
client = OpenAI(api_key="sk-...")

# After (through ONTO proxy — GOLD injected server-side):
client = OpenAI(
    api_key="onto_sk_...",
    base_url="https://api.ontostandard.org/v1/proxy",
    default_headers={"X-Provider-Key": "sk-...your_openai_key"}
)

# Everything else stays the same
response = client.chat.completions.create(
    model="gpt-4o",
    messages=[{"role": "user", "content": "Your question"}]
)
Other providers (DeepSeek, Groq, Mistral, etc.)
# Same endpoint, add X-Provider-Base-URL header:
client = OpenAI(
    api_key="onto_sk_...",
    base_url="https://api.ontostandard.org/v1/proxy",
    default_headers={
        "X-Provider-Key": "sk-...your_provider_key",
        "X-Provider-Base-URL": "https://api.deepseek.com/v1"
    }
)
cURL
curl -X POST https://api.ontostandard.org/v1/proxy/chat/completions \
  -H "x-api-key: onto_sk_..." \
  -H "X-Provider-Key: sk-...your_openai_key" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"model":"gpt-4o","messages":[{"role":"user","content":"Your question"}]}'
How it works: Your request → ONTO injects GOLD discipline into system prompt → forwards to your provider → returns enhanced response. ONTO never stores your prompts or responses. Standard provider token costs apply.
