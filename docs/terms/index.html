Terms of Service

Effective 2026-04-27 · ONTO Standards Council · ontostandard.org

01 The service

ONTO Standards Council operates a continuous attestation framework for AI inference. The framework consists of:

  • A public registry of attested AI models and their providers.
  • A signing protocol for inference signals (input hash, output hash, timestamp).
  • An open verifier that any party can run to check certificates.
  • The /agent/ demo — a free Bring-Your-Own-Key interface for evaluating the protocol.

Provider attestation rights (registering and signing for production AI models) are paid and governed by separate license agreements.

02 Acceptable use

  • Register only AI models you operate or have authorization to operate.
  • Transmit only signed signals that reflect genuine inference events.
  • Publish certificates honestly — do not edit, omit, or selectively republish.
  • Do not generate automated traffic intended to overwhelm the registry or verifier.

03 Prohibited

  • Reverse engineering of the signing protocol with intent to forge attestations.
  • Falsifying signals, including generating signed events that did not occur.
  • Misrepresenting attestation status — for example, claiming a model is attested when it is not, or claiming a current certificate when the latest one has been revoked.
  • Spoofing another provider's identity or impersonating the Council.

Violations may result in immediate revocation of attestation rights and public listing of the revocation.

04 Intellectual property

The ONTO Standard, the registry data structures, the signing protocol, and the verifier reference implementation are property of ONTO Standards Council. They are transferable to a designated successor entity under the governance rules described at /standard/governance/. This transferability is a deliberate design feature — the standard is intended to outlast any single organization.

Provider-submitted model metadata remains the provider's property. ONTO holds a non-exclusive license to publish it as part of attestation.
